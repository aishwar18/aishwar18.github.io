This application uses the convolutional neural network for classification of the images as ["AnnualCrop", "Forest", "HerbaceousVegetation", "Highway", "Industrial", "Pasture", "PermanentCrop","Residential", "River" and "SeaLake"] and suggests the user appropriate plan of action based on the previous data to achieve sustainable development. It tracks the changes in the particular piece of land over the years. On analysing the changes, it will be able to convey if it is a positive or negative development. Further using machine learning models, the application suggests plan of action for further sustainable development.






User :
Those who want to know about the land, it's history over the years can sign-in as a user and can view the basic details about the land by clicking the appropriate date and the coordinates of the place that they want to see..






Government Officials :
Government Officials can register themself by giving the relevant details about their designation, address and other things given in the form and once verified are allowed to see more details about the land and land owner provided given appropriate date and coordinates of the place..






Construction Agents :
Construction Agents can register themself by giving the relevant details about their designation, address and other things given in the form and once verified are allowed to see more details about the land and land owner provided given appropriate date and coordinates of the place..
